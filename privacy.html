Privacy Policy

Dope: Ballistic Log · Effective January 1, 2025

What data we collect

Dope collects no personal data. The app has no user accounts, analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or network connection. No data is transmitted from your device to any server.

Data stored on your device

All data you enter — rifle profiles, weather readings, shot logs, session notes — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

Optional tip purchases are processed entirely by Apple. Dope does not receive, store, or process any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all payment transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy

Third-party libraries

Dope includes jsPDF (MIT License) for PDF generation. jsPDF operates entirely on-device and transmits no data.

Children's privacy

Dope is rated 17+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
